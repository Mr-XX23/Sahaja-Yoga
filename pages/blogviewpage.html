The first chakra, named the Mooladhara Chakra is made of the earth element and it represents the start of life. For instance a person with a strong qualities at the Mooladhara center has a good inner sense of direction (in more ways than one). The basic quality of the Chakra is innocence, which is formed on the basis of every righteous character. This innocence is the type of quality, which is visible and expressed in babies and small children. It represents action without motive or desire for gross personal gain. The fundamental quality of the Mooladhara chakra is innocence. Innocence is the basis of righteous (dharmic) character. Your innocence can never be destroyed, but it may be covered (or masked) by your ego and conditionings, just as the sun may be covered by clouds.
